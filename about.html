Our Story

Local, native guides sharing the soul of Armenia through personal, unhurried journeys.

Built around your pace — not a bus schedule.

Upupa Travel was born from a simple belief: the best way to see a country is through the eyes of those who call it home. We moved away from the rigid structures of mass tourism to create a private guide service based in Yerevan that prioritizes depth over checklist-ticking.

Our philosophy centers on flexibility. Whether it's stopping for a spontaneous coffee in a mountain village or lingering an extra hour at a cliffside monastery to catch the perfect light, we ensure your journey is as unique as your interests.
